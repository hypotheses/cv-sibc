Good Old-fashioned Pancake

This is the "Good Old-fashioned Pancakes" that usually reminds us of the good time we have when we were young having pancakes with families, as good memory tastes like nothing on earth.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted

Cooking Instruction

  1. In a large bowl 🍜
  2. Sift togetherthe flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. 🍳
  3. Make a well in the center
  4. pour in the milk, egg and melted butter 🌧
  5. mix until smooth.
  6. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. 🔥🔥
  7. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. 🏆
  8. Brown on both sides and serve hot. 🥞
